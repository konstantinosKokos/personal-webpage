Hey!

I am Kokos (he/him) -- previously an engineer, currently a PhD candidate and soon to be on the lookout for work :)
I received my diploma in electrical & computer engineering from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. After graduation, I moved to Utrecht to pursue a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence. For the first half of my degree, I was also employed as a machine learning engineer at geekbot.io. For the latter half (and for four years now), I am working on my PhD under the supervision of prof. dr. Michael Moortgat in the NWO-funded project "A composition calculus for vector-based semantic modelling with a localization for Dutch". My current research revolves around type-driven methods for syntactic parsing and semantic compositionality. I am especially interested in the intersection of formal and data-driven methods; if that floats your boat rubber duck, you can have a look at my publications page.
