I am Kokos (he/him).

After 4+ years of working on my PhD under the supervision of Michael Moortgat, I recently submitted my dissertation titled Dependency as Modality, Parsing as Permutation: A Neurosymbolic Perspective on Categorial Grammars. You can find a pre-review version here. Previously, I received a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Utrecht University while working as a machine learning engineer at geekbot.io. Even previously, I received my diploma in Electrical & Computer Enginering from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki.

My research interests revolve around type-driven methods for syntactic parsing and semantic compositionality. I am especially interested in the intersection of formal and data-driven methods; if that floats your boat rubber duck, you can have a look at my publications page.
