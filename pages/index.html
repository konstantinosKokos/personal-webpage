Hey!

I am Kokos (he/him).

From July onwards I will have joined the group of Vikas Garg as a postdoctoral researcher in Aalto University. My research is to revolve around reinforcement learning and its applications on neurosymbolic systems.

I recently defended my thesis, officially joining the doctors' club. My dissertation, titled Dependency as Modality, Parsing as Permutation: A Neurosymbolic Perspective on Categorial Grammars presents a modal extension to type-logical grammars intended to capture dependency roles, and investigates the parsing-as-deduction paradigm from the angle of a machine learning practitioner. You can find a faithful copy of the physical print here. During my PhD, I received a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Utrecht University. Prior to that, I was working as a machine learning engineer at geekbot.io. Even previously, I received my diploma in Electrical & Computer Enginering from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki.

My published research revolves around structured machine learning, neurosymbolic reasoning, and type-driven approaches to syntactic parsing and semantic compositionality, among others. I am especially interested in the interface between formal and data-driven methods; if that floats your boat rubber duck, you can have a look at my publications page.
